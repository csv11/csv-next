
## Numbers

Supported styles:


Use underscore (`_`) or space (` `) for pretty-printing:

~~~
12_345_658
12 345 658
~~~

- Allow spaces? Why? Why not?


Short forms:

~~~
12m   -- 12 millions => 12_000_000
1m    -- 1 million => 1_000_000
12.4m  -- 12.4 millions => 12_400_000
~~~

~~~
10k   -- 10_000
10.7k -- 10_700
~~~
